Absolute Positioning

I am a basic block level element. My adjacent block level elemets sit on a line below me.

By default we span 100% of the width of our parent element, and we are as tall as our child content. Total width and height is content + padding + border width/height

We are separated by our margins. Because of margin collapsing, we are separated by the width of one of our margins, not both.
